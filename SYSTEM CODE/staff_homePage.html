Services for Staff

Academic and professional staff have full access to all student services, as well as specialised help from our librarians.

Subject Readings

To assign reading material and other resources to students in your subject, use the Subject Readings tool that integrates with Moodle. The Subject Readings guide can help you set up your list. You'll be able to start a new list or request a migration of previous resources.

Ordering new resources via Library SEARCH

Search for the resource in Library SEARCH. Ensure you select 'Show results outside UOW Library'.
Click the item's title.

Under 'How to get it', select one of the following:

  • Purchase Request form: to ask the Library to purchase a new item for the collection.
  • The relevant 'Get it' option: to either borrow a physical copy or get a digital chapter via Document Delivery. If there is no 'Get it' option, click the link to Document Delivery and fill in the blank request form.

* You can also request items for your reading list via the Subject Readings tool.
